I am Reza. Welcome to my homepage!

I do research at the intersection of computer vision, computer graphics, and deep learning. For the most part, I am interested in building systems that can capture, comprehend, and yield dynamic reconstruction of objects in 3D from everyday videos and photos. The recent advancement of 3D AR/VR technologies provides with ample information about surrounding objects in terms of geometry, shape, and scale. The goal of my research is to leverage these powerful 3D representations to assist in mutual human-computer interaction.

Currently I am working as a research assistant at the Cyber Physical Systems research group led by Prof. Dr. Matthias Althoff in TU Munich. I was fortunate to work under supervision of Prof. Dr. Gudrun Johanna Klinker during my M.Sc. thesis, where my research focuses on 3D point cloud analysis exploiting 3D deep learning.
